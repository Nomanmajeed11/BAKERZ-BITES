>Chiffon Cake<

Known for its light and airy texture, Chiffon Cake is a delicate dessert made with oil, eggs, sugar, flour, and baking powder. The addition of egg whites beaten to stiff peaks gives the cake its characteristic loftiness and tenderness. Chiffon Cake's versatility allows for a myriad of flavor variations, from classic vanilla to zesty lemon or decadent chocolate. Whether enjoyed plain or with a drizzle of glaze, Chiffon Cake is a delightful treat that satisfies with its ethereal texture and subtle sweetness.
