>Tiramisu<

Hailing from Italy, Tiramisu is a sophisticated dessert beloved for its delicate layers and rich flavors. This exquisite creation features ladyfingers soaked in espresso and layered with a velvety mixture of mascarpone cheese, sugar, and egg yolks. The dessert is then dusted with cocoa powder, imparting a subtle bitterness that balances the sweetness. Tiramisu's combination of coffee, cream, and chocolate makes it an indulgent treat that epitomizes Italian culinary finesse.
