>Cheesecake<

Originating in ancient Greece but perfected in the United States, Cheesecake is a decadent dessert made with cream cheese, eggs, and sugar on a graham cracker or cookie crust. The creamy filling is often flavored with vanilla or citrus zest and baked until set. Whether served plain or adorned with fruit compote, chocolate ganache, or caramel sauce, Cheesecake is a versatile treat that appeals to a wide range of palates. Its luscious texture and rich flavor make it a beloved indulgence for special occasions.
