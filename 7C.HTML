>Opera Cake<

A French classic, Opera Cake is a sophisticated dessert that showcases layers of almond sponge cake soaked in coffee syrup. Between the layers, you'll find a luxurious combination of coffee buttercream and chocolate ganache, adding depth and richness to every bite. The cake is finished with a glossy chocolate glaze, creating a stunning presentation that reflects its refined flavors. Opera Cake is a true masterpiece of French patisserie, celebrated for its elegant taste and impeccable craftsmanship.
