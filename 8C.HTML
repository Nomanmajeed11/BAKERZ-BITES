>Sacher Torte<

Originating from Austria, Sacher Torte is a beloved dessert characterized by its dense chocolate sponge cake layered with apricot jam. The cake is traditionally coated in a smooth chocolate glaze, imparting a luxurious finish. Sacher Torte's contrasting flavors of rich chocolate and tangy apricot create a harmonious balance that delights the palate. Often served with a dollop of whipped cream, this indulgent cake is a symbol of Austrian culinary heritage.
