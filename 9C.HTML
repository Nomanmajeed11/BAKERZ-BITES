>Victoria Sponge Cake<

A quintessential British treat, Victoria Sponge Cake is a simple yet elegant dessert that embodies timeless charm. This light and fluffy sponge cake are sandwiched together with layers of raspberry jam and freshly whipped cream. The cake's delicate crumb and subtle sweetness allow the flavors of the jam and cream to shine through. Adorned with a dusting of powdered sugar, Victoria Sponge Cake is a delightful accompaniment to afternoon tea or any celebratory occasion.
